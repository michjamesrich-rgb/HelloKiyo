## HelloKiyo launch + growth plan (starter)

### Month 1 objective
- Target: **$5,000 profit**
- Primary levers: conversion rate, box tier mix, repeat purchases, and keeping landed-cost estimates conservative.

### Content pipeline (Kiyo + Michael)
Create a repeatable weekly loop:
- **2–3 short videos/week** (TikTok + IG Reels + YouTube Shorts)
  - “Fill the box with me” (gauge fills + satisfying adds)
  - “Japan vibe of the week” (theme: pink nails, glow skincare, kawaii charms)
  - “Mission challenge” (mix & match categories to unlock a bonus)
- **1 long video/week** (YouTube)
  - unboxing + favorites + “what I’d put in my next box”
- **Daily stories** (IG)
  - polls: “Sweet vs Savory”, “Pink vs Teal”, “Nails vs Skincare”

### Paid vs organic (starter)
- Start **organic-first** until the funnel is stable.
- Then small tests:
  - retarget site visitors
  - promote top-performing “fill the gauge” creatives

### On-site growth mechanics
- Limited “drops” (weekly refresh of a few items) to create urgency.
- Missions/bonuses to encourage variety and completion.
- Post-purchase: “build your next box” prompt + discount later (Phase 2).

### Analytics to watch daily
- Box tier selection rate
- Add-to-box rate
- Checkout click rate
- Purchase conversion (when Shopify integration is live)
- Gauge completion distribution (are users hitting the limit too early?)
- Mission unlock rate
